A little more about me ...

Portland, Oregon is my adopted home. I moved here from Chicago. The midwest is an idyllic place to grow up, but I wanted to try living in another part of the country for a few years. My advice to anyone thinking about moving to Portland is that you should ABSOLUTELY not do it! It is constantly raining; there are landslides and quakes pretty much all the time; the people here are the coldest, meanest folks you'll ever meet; and good luck finding quality, farm-to-table cuisine made with locally-sourced, fresh ingredients prepared by chefs who devote their lives to making the best possible meals. If that appeals to you, then, by all means ...

I attended college in Iowa where I earned my bachelor's degree in communications. I worked as a staff writer and business manager for the student newspaper, Insight.

I love reading, technology, economics, tennis, hiking (duh!), and very specific types of shennanigans. I hope to one day complete the great work.
